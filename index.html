About Pokepedia

Learn about all of your favorite pokemon's strengths, weaknesses, and backgrounds. We have a fully interactive pokedex with many different features for all users. Core Features Include: Pokemon Lookup, Team Generation, Comparing Pokemon

Real Time Lookup

With our real time lookup, you can find information on any pokemon in the selected region. Learn about each pokemon's background, stats, moveset, and evolution chain with one click.

Team Generation

Automatically generate a full team of six pokemon as you please. You can customize the generator with options such as banning legendary pokemon, allowing duplicate pokemon, allowing more than one starter pokemon, and much more.
